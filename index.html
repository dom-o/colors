Colors

This is a tool for finding distinct colors/groups of colors.

I calculate the difference between groups as the average of the differences between each color, that is, for two groups of three colors, I find the difference between the two first colors of each group, the difference between the two second colors, and the difference between the two third colors, and average them.

You can weight the average by mucking around with the sliders. The further right you slide, the more important the difference between the colors in that position will be to the overall difference. If you're looking for difference between single colors/color groups of size 1, the weight doesn't matter but you can still muck around with the slider anyway.
